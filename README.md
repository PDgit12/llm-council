# 🧠 The AI Council (Elite 4 Edition)

> **"Maximum Intelligence. Zero Bloat."**

The **AI Council** is a high-performance multi-agent system that uses a team of 4 specialized AI models to solve complex tasks better than any single model could alone.

Instead of relying on one general-purpose assistant (like ChatGPT), the Council routes your request to specific "Specialists" who excel at that exact type of problem—whether it's coding, creative writing, or deep reasoning.

---

## 🧐 What is it? (The "Council of 5" Architecture)
The **LLM Council** leverages **5 Unique AI Models**, each with a distinct personality and capability set, to ensure no angle is missed.

---

## 💡 Why use this?
Single models have weaknesses. The Council eliminates them by working together:

1.  **Speed vs. Quality:** 
    *   *Problem:* Smart models are slow. Fast models are dumb.
    *   *Council Solution:* We use **Nemotron** for speed (coding) and **Gemma** for smarts (reasoning), giving you the best of both worlds.

2.  **Hallucinations:** 
    *   *Problem:* AI sometimes lies.
    *   *Council Solution:* **The Guardian (Trinity)** double-checks every major claim, significantly reducing errors.

3.  **Niche Knowledge:** 
    *   *Problem:* General models don't know deep technical details.
    *   *Council Solution:* **The Speedster** is a specialist in technical/coding tasks, providing expert-level answers.
